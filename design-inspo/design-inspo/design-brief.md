# DESIGN BRIEF — Linguaenlinea 2026

## Brand Identity
- Platform: Linguaenlinea 2026
- Tagline: "El mundo de Linguaenlinea"
- Existing site: linguaenlinea.eu
- Languages: Spanish, Dutch, English

## Visual Style
- Premium Dark Mode (background #0D0D0D)
- NOT pure black — warm dark with depth
- Primary accent: Coral Latino (#FF6B6B)
- Secondary accent: Gold (#FFB800)
- Success color: Verde (#00C896)
- Typography: Plus Jakarta Sans (UI), 
  Fraunces (headings — warm, literary feel)

## Avatar System (KEY DIFFERENTIATOR)
- Custom Latin American characters guide 
  the student through lessons
- Each avatar represents a different 
  cultural region and topic:
  * Cuban female → Phonetics & Music
  * Argentine male → Culture & Travel  
  * Mexican female → Food & Daily life
  * Dutch-Latino → Dutch Track guide
- Avatars appear in lessons, give feedback,
  celebrate progress
- Style: Modern flat illustration, 
  expressive, diverse, warm

## UI Patterns
- Card-based lesson map (like Duolingo 
  but darker and more sophisticated)
- Glassmorphism cards for lesson content
- Micro-animations on quiz answers
- Progress rings instead of progress bars
- Streak system with fire emoji 🔥

## Reference Apps
- Duolingo: lesson map structure 
  and gamification
- Notion: clean dark layout
- Spotify: card browsing and content discovery
- Headspace: calm onboarding flow

## What to AVOID
- Bright white backgrounds
- Generic stock photos
- Clipart-style illustrations
- Busy layouts with too many elements

## Mood Board Keywords
- Sophisticated, warm, Latin richness
- Modern and global
- Simple but memorable
- Cultural pride without stereotypes
